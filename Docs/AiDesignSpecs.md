Connect Africa AI Expander Design

Philosophy

The Expander exists to enhance reading, not replace the encyclopedia.

The Expander is an intelligent reading companion that extends articles while respecting the Connect Africa Knowledge Base as the authoritative source.

It must never modify the encyclopedia.

It must never create or publish knowledge.

It only generates temporary reading material.

---

Expansion Levels

Level 1

Internal Knowledge Expansion

The AI first searches the Connect Africa database.

If related articles exist:

- Read those articles.
- Read related entities.
- Read connected relationships.
- Read ontology information.
- Read timeline events.

The generated expansion is based primarily on Connect Africa's own verified knowledge.

The original articles are never copied.

Instead, the AI produces a concise continuation written in the same narrative style.

---

Example

Reader is reading

History of Timbuktu

Reader asks

Expand on the Sankore University.

Connect Africa already contains

Sankore University

The Expander:

- Reads the article.
- Summarizes the relevant knowledge.
- Writes approximately 200 words that continue naturally from the current article.
- Displays:

«Learn more in the full article:
Sankore University»

The reader can continue reading immediately or open the dedicated article.

---

Level 2

External Knowledge Expansion

If Connect Africa has no article on the requested topic:

The Expander performs temporary external research.

Sources may include:

- Academic publications
- Government publications
- Museums
- Universities
- Trusted historical archives
- Verified public sources

The AI generates approximately 200 words.

The generated text:

- is temporary,
- is never indexed,
- is never stored in the public knowledge base.

---

Level 3

Personal Research

Authenticated users may choose

Save to My Research

The generated expansion is stored only inside the user's personal workspace.

It becomes:

My Research

My Notes

Saved Expansions

This data:

- belongs only to the user,
- is private,
- is not searchable publicly,
- is not part of Connect Africa's encyclopedia.

---

Writing Rules

The Expander must never behave like a chatbot.

Forbidden:

Here are five facts...

As an AI...

According to my research...

I found...

Instead, every expansion must read exactly like the encyclopedia article continues.

Example

...

The migration continued throughout the nineteenth century.

Further archaeological evidence suggests that settlements gradually expanded into neighboring valleys, where trade routes connected several independent communities. Excavations indicate that...

The reader should not perceive a transition between the original article and the generated continuation.

---

Related Knowledge

Whenever internal knowledge exists, the Expander should recommend additional reading.

Example

Related Reading

Kingdom of Mali

Sankore University

Trans-Saharan Trade

Islamic Scholarship

These are recommendations only.

The generated expansion remains independent.

---

Maximum Generation

Each expansion is limited to:

Approximately 200 words

Long-form responses are intentionally avoided.

The objective is to encourage exploration of the encyclopedia.

---

Source Priority

The Expander must prioritize information in this order.

1.
Connect Africa Knowledge Base

2.
Verified Sources attached to Connect Africa

3.
Trusted External Sources

4.
Large Language Model reasoning

The internal knowledge base is always considered the primary source.

---

AI Restrictions

The Expander may:

✓ Read articles

✓ Read entities

✓ Read ontology

✓ Read relationships

✓ Read timelines

✓ Read verified sources

✓ Generate temporary continuations

✓ Recommend related articles

✓ Save user research

The Expander may not:

✗ Publish articles

✗ Edit articles

✗ Create entities

✗ Create ontology

✗ Modify relationships

✗ Write into the encyclopedia

✗ Affect search indexes

✗ Alter production data

---

User Experience Goal

The reader should feel they are simply continuing to read the encyclopedia.

The experience must resemble seamless article continuation rather than interacting with a conversational AI.

The AI remains invisible.

Connect Africa remains the focus.

---

Future Enhancements

Future versions may support:
